📝 Notes


Prices are researched retail (July 2026). NGO/bulk discounts may reduce costs by 15–30%.

Mediterranean SAR context assumed — items rated for marine environment (saltwater, UV, humidity).

Flares have 3–4 year shelf life; rotate stock.

Lifejacket recommendation: use cheap foam vests for rescued people (no maintenance, no training), 150N+ auto-inflatable for crew.

Mobile ultrasound is a luxury but genuinely useful for onboard FAST exams and triage.

The SAR drone with thermal is the single biggest force multiplier for finding people in water — can cover in 20 minutes what a boat covers in 2 hours.
